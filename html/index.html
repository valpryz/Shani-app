bataillon des entrepreneurs panafricains

Learn inbound marketing techniques that range from content creation to social promotion to converting and nurturing leads and beyond. By the end of this certification course, you'll be well on your way to building your inbound marketing strategy.

details de la formation

  • 4 modules
  • 15 lecons
  • 7 videos
  • 4 quizzes
  • 3hr14 minutes completion time

avantages de la formation

  • Learn about inbound marketing techniques
  • Develop understanding of core tactics of inbound marketing
  • Advance your skills as an inbound marketer
